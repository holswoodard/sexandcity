Sex & the City: Boyfriend Edition

By Holliday Woodard


"Sex and the City" is a groundbreaking television series that first aired in 1998 and quickly became a cultural phenomenon. Set in New York City, the show follows the lives of four women—Carrie Bradshaw, Samantha Jones, Charlotte York, and Miranda Hobbes—as they navigate relationships, careers, and the complexities of modern womanhood. At its core, "Sex and the City" explores themes of love, friendship, and empowerment, breaking social norms surrounding female sexuality and challenging traditional gender roles.
